To disconnect your ZEIT Now account from Bitbucket, take the following steps:

First, log into [Bitbucket](https://bitbucket.org/). Then, click on your profile icon on the bottom left.

If you've installed ZEIT Now for your **personal** Bitbucket account, select **Bitbucket settings**.

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/bitbucket-settings.png`}
  width={407 / 2}
  height={451 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>Bitbucket Settings.</Caption>

Or, if you've installed ZEIT Now for your **team** Bitbucket account, first click on the team name:

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/bitbucket-team.png`}
  width={253 / 2}
  height={136 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>Your Bitbucket Team.</Caption>

Then, click on **Settings**:

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/bitbucket-team-settings.png`}
  width={424 / 2}
  height={555 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>Bitbucket Team Settings.</Caption>

#### Revoke access for ZEIT Now

Next, from **Access Management**, select **OAuth**.

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/bitbucket-oauth.png`}
  width={449 / 2}
  height={242 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>OAuth Settings.</Caption>

Finally, from **OAuth integrated applications**, locate **ZEIT Now** and click the **Revoke** link next to it.

<Image
  src={`${process.env.ASSETS}/docs/git-integrations/uninstall-now-bitbucket.png`}
  width={934 / 2}
  height={374 / 2}
  shadow
  borderRadius
/>
<Caption>Revoking access.</Caption>

After disconnecting, you can always reconnect your ZEIT Now account to a Bitbucket account again.
